About Melton Cape Town

Melton Cape Town Adult Jewish Education is dedicated to providing high-quality, text-based courses that cover a wide range of topics including Jewish history, philosophy, literature, and more. Our expert faculty offers both online and in-person classes to cater to the needs of our diverse community.

Founded in the heart of Cape Town, we have become a trusted hub for adult Jewish education. Our approach is rooted in tradition and scholarship, aiming to enrich lives through the exploration of Jewish texts and ideas.

At Melton, we believe in lifelong learning and are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment for all. Our courses are designed to inspire, challenge, and engage learners of all backgrounds and levels of experience.
