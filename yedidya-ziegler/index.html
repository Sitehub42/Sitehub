About Yedidya Ziegler

I am Yedidya Ziegler, a passionate chess player and mentor. My journey with chess began as a young enthusiast, captivated by the strategic depth of the game. Today, I share this passion by teaching chess to beginners, kids, teens, and anyone eager to learn.

Chess is more than a game to me; it's a tool for teaching valuable life lessons. Through my coaching, I aim to instill discipline, strategic thinking, and patience in my students. My sessions are designed to be engaging and insightful, ensuring that each student not only learns the moves but the mindset behind them.

My promise is to deliver personalized and meaningful mentoring that goes beyond just chess. I am committed to helping my students grow both on and off the board, providing them with the skills to face life's challenges with confidence.
