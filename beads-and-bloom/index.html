About Beads & Bloom

At Beads & Bloom, we believe in the power of handmade artistry to uplift small businesses.

Beads & Bloom's mission is to empower spaza shops and micro-retailers by providing unique, high-quality handmade beaded jewellery. We believe in fostering growth for small businesses through accessible and attractive products, emphasizing client success and agile methodologies in our craft.

We are passionate about creating beautiful, artisanal accessories that resonate with diverse customer bases, helping our partners thrive in the competitive retail landscape. Our commitment to quality and innovative design ensures that every piece tells a story of craftsmanship and dedication.
