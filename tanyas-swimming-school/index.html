About Us

At Tanya's Swimming School, we believe every child deserves to feel safe and confident in the water. Our mission is to empower children with essential swimming skills, fostering confidence, safety, and a lifelong love for the water.

Child-Focused Approach

We specialize in teaching kids, from the very basics of water familiarity to advanced stroke training, ensuring a fun and supportive learning environment.

Proven Success

Our methodologies emphasize practical skills, safety, and continuous improvement, leading to confident and skilled young swimmers.

Effective & Fun Learning

We use engaging and agile teaching techniques, making lessons enjoyable and highly effective for children of all ages and skill levels.

Our Services

Tanya's Swimming School provides comprehensive swimming programs designed to cater to every child's needs, whether they are just starting out or looking to refine their technique.

Beginner Lessons

Introducing children to water safety, basic water confidence, floating, and fundamental swimming movements.

Advanced Training

Focus on stroke correction, endurance building, and advanced techniques for proficient swimmers looking to improve.

Individual Classes

Personalized one-on-one instruction tailored to the child's specific needs, pace, and learning style.

Group Sessions

Learn and grow with peers in a fun, social, and motivating group environment, suitable for various skill levels.
