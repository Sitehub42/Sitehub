About Tanya's Swimming School

Our mission at Tanya's Swimming School is to provide a safe, fun, and effective learning environment for children to develop essential swimming skills.

We specialize in teaching kids to swim, from the very basics to advanced training, focusing on individual progress and group dynamics. Our agile methodologies ensure adaptable and effective lessons, leading to measurable client success and confident swimmers. We believe that learning to swim is a vital life skill, and we are dedicated to making the journey enjoyable and rewarding for every child.

Our Services

At Tanya's Swimming School, we offer comprehensive swimming programs tailored for all skill levels, ensuring every child gains confidence and proficiency in the water.

Individual Lessons

Personalized one-on-one coaching focused on your child's specific needs and pace, ensuring rapid progress and skill mastery.

Group Classes

Engaging group sessions that promote social interaction and learning, perfect for building confidence in a fun, supportive environment.

Beginner Basics

Essential water safety, breath control, floating, and fundamental stroke techniques for children new to swimming.

Advanced Training

For more experienced swimmers, focusing on stroke refinement, endurance building, and competitive techniques.
