About Us

At Tanya's Swimming School, our mission is to provide high-quality swimming instruction, fostering a love for water and building confident swimmers from basics to advanced training. We specialize in teaching kids to swim, offering both individual and group classes tailored to various skill levels. Our approach emphasizes client success through personalized attention and agile methodologies, ensuring every child feels supported and progresses at their own pace. Join us to experience a fun, safe, and effective learning environment where every splash leads to confidence!

Our Services

We offer comprehensive swimming programs tailored for all ages and skill levels, ensuring a safe and fun learning environment.

Beginner Classes (Kids)

Starting with the basics, we introduce young swimmers to water safety, breath control, and fundamental strokes in a playful and encouraging setting.

Group Sessions

Learn alongside peers in our dynamic group classes, fostering teamwork and motivation while receiving expert instruction.

Private Coaching

For personalized attention and accelerated progress, our one-on-one sessions cater specifically to your child's learning style and goals.

Stroke Development

Focus on refining existing strokes and learning new ones, enhancing technique, efficiency, and overall swimming performance.

Water Safety & Survival

Essential lessons on water safety, self-rescue techniques, and emergency procedures to ensure confidence and competence in aquatic environments.

Advanced Training

For those looking to compete or further excel, we offer advanced training sessions focusing on endurance, speed, and competitive strategies.
