About Nicky

A different kind of health conversation

I'm Nicky Taylor — a passionate nutritional therapist with a deep commitment to helping women understand and heal their bodies. My own health journey taught me that symptoms are signals, not sentences. That belief shapes everything I do.

I work with women navigating gut health, hormonal imbalances, menopause, chronic fatigue, and more — providing personalised support at every stage of their journey. I bring evidence-based nutrition together with genuine compassion and curiosity.

My practice is built around one core belief: when you understand what your body is telling you, you have the power to change it.

My Philosophy

What I believe about healing

01

Root Cause, Not Band-Aids

Symptoms are your body asking for help. I dig deeper to find the underlying imbalances — because masking symptoms doesn't create lasting change.

02

You Are Unique

No two women are the same. Your history, genetics, environment, and lifestyle all matter. My approach is always deeply personal and never one-size-fits-all.

03

The Whole Person

Physical symptoms rarely exist in isolation. I look at the whole picture — gut, hormones, stress, sleep, and mindset — to support you fully.

04

Evidence & Intuition

I blend the latest nutritional science with a genuine listening ear. The best outcomes come from rigorous knowledge and genuine connection.
