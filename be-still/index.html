About Be Still

I founded Be Still to help individuals navigate life's challenges with grace and resilience. My journey began with a passion for understanding human emotions and supporting others in their personal growth. With a background in counseling and life coaching, I aim to provide tools and strategies that empower individuals to live their fullest lives.

At Be Still, I focus on a trauma-informed approach, offering workshops and custom-designed toolkits that cater to specific emotional needs. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, grief, or the stress of daily life, I am here to guide you through it with compassion and understanding. My mission is to create a safe space where you can explore your feelings and develop strategies for well-being.

Integrity and empathy are at the core of my practice. I promise to provide personalized support and resources that address your unique needs. Through my services, I strive to foster a community of individuals who are not only surviving but thriving, equipped with the knowledge and confidence to face any challenge.
