About Paw Paw Pals

At Paw Paw Pals, our mission is to provide exceptional, compassionate dog grooming services that prioritize the health, happiness, and well-being of every furry client. We combine expertise with a gentle touch to ensure a positive and stress-free grooming experience, making tails wag and owners smile.

We believe in personalized care, understanding that each dog has unique needs and temperaments. Our experienced groomers use only high-quality, pet-friendly products and employ agile methodologies in our approach, meaning we adapt our techniques to suit your pet's comfort and specific requirements, ensuring client success through happy, healthy pets.

Our Services

Paw Paw Pals offers a comprehensive range of professional and caring grooming services designed to keep your dog looking, feeling, and smelling fantastic. From a quick tidy-up to a luxurious full pamper session, we've got your furry friend covered.

Full Groom

Includes bath, brush, haircut, blow dry, nail trim, and ear cleaning for a complete transformation.

Bath & Tidy

Perfect for in-between grooms, this includes a revitalizing bath, thorough brush-out, and nail trim.

De-Shedding Treatment

Specialized treatment to reduce shedding, leaving your dog's coat healthier and your home cleaner.

Nail Trimming

Keep your dog's paws healthy and comfortable with our professional nail trimming service.

Paw Pad & Ear Cleaning

Essential maintenance for cleanliness and hygiene, reducing the risk of discomfort or infection.
