About Healing hands

My name is Nicky Newfield, and I am passionate about helping individuals transform into the healthiest versions of themselves through reflexology and alternative healing practices. With years of expertise, my approach is holistic and personalized, ensuring each session is tailored to your unique needs.

The story of Healing hands began with my own journey of self-discovery and healing. After experiencing the profound benefits of reflexology, I dedicated my life to mastering the art and science of this ancient practice. Today, I use a variety of alternative healing tools to guide my clients towards balance and well-being.

At Healing hands, my promise is to create a safe and nurturing space where you have the permission to be present and heal in your own way. I believe in empowering you with the tools and knowledge to take charge of your health journey.
