About Us

Located in the heart of Claremont, Cape Town, WEST Physiotherapy is dedicated to providing top-notch physiotherapy services for clients of all ages. Our experienced team is committed to helping you regain your strength and mobility through personalized care and attention.

Our journey began with a shared passion for improving the physical wellbeing of our community. We employ a hands-on approach, ensuring each client receives tailored treatment plans that cater to their specific needs. This personal touch is what sets us apart in the field of physiotherapy.

At WEST Physiotherapy, we value trust and transparency. We believe in building long-lasting relationships with our clients, rooted in mutual respect and effective communication. Our promise is to guide you every step of the way on your journey to optimal physical health.
