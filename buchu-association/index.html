About Buchu Association

The Buchu Association is dedicated to promoting and supporting the growth of a sustainable buchu industry in South Africa. Our collective includes a diverse range of stakeholders who are passionate about the cultivation and processing of this unique indigenous plant.

Our story began over three centuries ago when the unique properties of buchu were first discovered. Today, we continue to uphold the values of those who pioneered the industry, ensuring that the benefits of buchu reach markets across the globe.

We promise to protect the integrity of the buchu plant and its industry, ensuring that all stakeholders are included in the growth and prosperity of this sector. Our commitment to sustainability and quality guides everything we do.

What is Buchu?

Buchu is an indigenous plant from the Western Cape, renowned for its fragrance and health benefits. Our association educates on its rich history and uses.
