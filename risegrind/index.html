About Rise&Grind

Rise&Grind is a beloved coffee shop nestled in the vibrant community of Sunningdale, Cape Town. We pride ourselves on serving artisan coffee and delectable snacks that invigorate your senses and start your day on a high note.

Our journey began with a passion for exceptional coffee and a dream to create a space where the community could gather, relax, and connect. Every cup we serve is brewed with love and precision, ensuring a memorable experience for every visitor.

We are committed to using quality ingredients sourced locally whenever possible. Our promise is to deliver warmth and excellence in every interaction, providing a haven for coffee enthusiasts and newcomers alike.
