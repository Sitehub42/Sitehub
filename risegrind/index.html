About Rise&Grind

Rise&Grind is your neighborhood coffee shop where the art of coffee meets the taste of home. Located in the heart of Sunningdale, Cape Town, we serve a diverse community of coffee enthusiasts, offering a welcoming space to savor every sip.

Since our inception, Rise&Grind has been dedicated to bringing the freshest coffee and delightful snacks to our patrons. Our journey began with a passion for coffee and a desire to create a cozy spot where people can relax, recharge, and reconnect.

We believe in quality, community, and sustainability. Our promise is to deliver exceptional flavors and friendly service while supporting local suppliers and fostering a love for coffee culture in our vibrant community.
