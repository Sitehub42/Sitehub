About Rise&Grind

Rise&Grind is a local coffee shop nestled in the heart of Sunningdale, Cape Town. We are dedicated to serving our community with the finest coffee and a range of delicious snacks. Whether you're looking to relax with a book or catch up with friends, our cozy atmosphere is the perfect setting.

Our journey began with a passion for coffee and a commitment to quality. We believe in sourcing the best beans and crafting each cup with care. From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a beloved spot for locals and visitors alike, known for our friendly service and inviting space.

At Rise&Grind, we value community, quality, and sustainability. We are committed to providing a welcoming environment where everyone can enjoy a moment of peace with their favorite brew. Join us and experience the warmth and flavor that make us unique.
