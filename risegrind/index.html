About Rise&Grind

Located in the heart of Sunningdale, Cape Town, Rise&Grind is your local coffee shop offering a cozy atmosphere and the best coffee in town. Whether you're stopping by for a quick pick-me-up or settling in for a relaxing afternoon, our welcoming space is perfect for all.

Founded by a team of coffee enthusiasts, Rise&Grind is dedicated to serving the finest blends and freshly made snacks. Our passion for quality and attention to detail is evident in every cup we brew and every snack we prepare.

At Rise&Grind, we believe in creating a community hub where our customers feel at home. Our friendly staff is committed to providing exceptional service and ensuring that every visit is a delightful experience.
