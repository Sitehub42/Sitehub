About African Tails

African Tails is dedicated to the rescue and adoption of animals in Cape Town, South Africa. We provide care and shelter for stray and abandoned animals, ensuring they receive the attention they need before finding their forever homes.

Founded with a deep love for animals, our organization has grown into a community-supported initiative that brings together animal lovers and volunteers dedicated to making a difference. We strive to create a nurturing environment where animals can recover and thrive.

Our mission is to provide lasting change for animals in need. We believe in transparency, compassion, and dedication to the animals we serve, and we promise to maintain the highest standards of care and integrity in all our efforts.

Our Services

Explore the compassionate services we offer to the community and the animals we serve.

Animal Rescue

Our team is committed to rescuing animals in distress, providing them with immediate care, and ensuring they have a safe place to recover.

Adoptions

We facilitate the adoption process, matching animals with loving families who will provide them with a stable and nurturing environment.
