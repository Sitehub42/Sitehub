About Buyk It Fixed

Our Mission

At Buyk It Fixed, our mission is to provide top-quality, reliable, and friendly bicycle repair services to the Glenhazel community. We're passionate about keeping you on two wheels, ensuring every ride is safe and enjoyable. We believe in transparency, honesty, and getting you back on your bike as quickly as possible.

Our Expertise

With years of experience in the bicycle repair industry, we pride ourselves on our deep understanding of all bike types, from road bikes to mountain bikes and everything in between. We constantly stay updated with the latest cycling technology and repair techniques. Client success is at the heart of what we do; we listen to your needs and apply agile methodologies to diagnose and resolve issues efficiently, minimizing your downtime.

Our commitment to excellence ensures that every repair is performed with meticulous attention to detail, using only quality parts and tools. We're not just fixing bikes; we're building lasting relationships with our cycling community.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of bicycle repair services to keep your ride in peak condition. From routine maintenance to complex repairs, we've got you covered.

Tune-ups & Maintenance

Keep your bike running smoothly with our comprehensive tune-ups, covering adjustments, lubrication, and safety checks.

Flat Tire Repair

Quick and reliable flat tire repair to get you back on the road in no time. We handle punctures and tire replacements efficiently.

Brake Adjustments & Repair

Ensure your safety with perfectly calibrated brakes. We handle adjustments, pad replacements, and full system repairs.

Gear Servicing

Smooth shifting is essential. We'll adjust, clean, and repair your drivetrain for optimal performance and a seamless ride.

Wheel Truing

Wobbly wheels? Our experts can true your wheels for a more stable, efficient, and safer ride, reducing wear and tear.

Accessory Installation

From lights to racks, child seats to computers, we can professionally install all your bicycle accessories with care.
