About Sunshine's Treats and Eats

At Sunshine's Treats and Eats, we are passionate about bringing joy to our community through our delicious bakery offerings. Nestled in Constantia, NY, our bakery serves locals and visitors alike with a wide range of delectable treats.

Our journey began with a simple love for baking and a desire to share that love with others. We pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients to create baked goods that not only taste amazing but also bring a smile to your face.

We believe in creating a warm and welcoming environment for all our customers. Our promise is to deliver top-notch quality and unparalleled service, ensuring that every visit to Sunshine's Treats and Eats is a delightful experience.
