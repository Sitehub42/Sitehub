About Sunshine's Treats and Eats

At Sunshine's Treats and Eats, we are passionate about creating delightful bakery items that bring joy to our local community in Constantia, NY. Our bakery is a haven for those who appreciate the art of baking and the taste of high-quality, handcrafted treats.

Founded by a team of dedicated bakers, Sunshine's Treats and Eats started as a small local bakery with a big dream: to create the most delicious and unique bakery items. Our approach is simple — we use only the finest ingredients, and every item is crafted with meticulous attention to detail.

We believe in the power of quality and community. Our promise to our customers is to provide bakery items that are not only delicious but also made with love and care. Visit us for an unforgettable bakery experience.
