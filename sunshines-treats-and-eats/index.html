About Sunshine's Treats and Eats

Sunshine's Treats and Eats is your local bakery in Constantia, NY, where we serve up fresh, homemade treats and beverages daily. Our mission is to bring a little sunshine into your day with every bite. From sweet pastries to savory breads, everything is crafted with love and attention to detail.

Founded by a family who loves to bake, our bakery has grown from a small kitchen into a beloved local destination. Our approach is simple: use the finest ingredients to create delicious, feel-good food. We believe in the power of community and the joy that comes from sharing good food with good friends.

At Sunshine's Treats and Eats, we value quality, integrity, and the happiness of our customers. We promise to always provide you with delightful, handcrafted products and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're stopping by for a quick coffee or ordering a custom cake, we're here to make your day a little brighter.
