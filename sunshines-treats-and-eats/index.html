About Sunshine's Treats and Eats

Sunshine's Treats and Eats is a charming bakery nestled in the heart of Constantia, NY. We pride ourselves in serving our local community with the freshest and most delightful baked goods. Whether you're craving a sweet treat or a hearty bite, we have something to satisfy every taste.

Our journey began with a passion for baking and a love for bringing joy to others through food. With a dedication to quality and a sprinkle of creativity, we have crafted a menu that celebrates both classic flavors and innovative creations. Our bakery is a haven for those who appreciate the art of baking and the joy of sharing it with loved ones.

At Sunshine's Treats and Eats, we believe in the power of community and the joy of giving back. Our values are rooted in trust, quality, and customer satisfaction. We promise to always provide our customers with a warm welcome and a delicious experience that will leave them coming back for more.
