About Le Petit Cafe

Le Petit Cafe is a cozy and welcoming cafe located in the heart of West Beach, Cape Town. We serve a diverse menu that caters to all tastes, including vegetarian options, and offer a variety of food products like freshly brewed coffee, delicious burgers, and toasted cheese sandwiches.

Founded by passionate food enthusiasts, Le Petit Cafe prides itself on offering a casual dining experience where customers can enjoy high-quality food and drinks in a relaxed environment. Our story began with a love for great coffee and a desire to create a space where everyone feels at home.

Our mission is to provide a family-friendly, LGBTQ+ and transgender safe space with amenities like free Wi-Fi, wheelchair-accessible parking, and dog-friendly policies. We’re dedicated to ensuring that every guest leaves satisfied and looking forward to their next visit.
