About

Lindi Duvenage Physiotherapy is dedicated to providing personalized physiotherapy services to business owners and professionals in the Val De Vie Winelands Lifestyle Estate area. Our focus is on helping individuals maintain their physical health and well-being, enabling them to thrive in their demanding careers.

Founded by Lindi Duvenage, our practice is built on a foundation of trust, expertise, and a genuine commitment to our clients' health. Lindi's approach combines traditional techniques with modern innovations to deliver effective treatments tailored to each client's unique needs.

We believe in creating a welcoming and supportive environment where clients feel comfortable and cared for. Our promise is to provide quality care, ensuring every treatment session contributes positively to your overall health journey.
