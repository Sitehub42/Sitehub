About Us

Our mission is to efficiently reunite individuals with their lost possessions within the Kann House, fostering a community of trust and responsibility.

At Lost and Found, we specialize in managing and tracking items lost and found within the Kann House. Our expertise lies in creating seamless processes for item registration, secure storage, and efficient retrieval. We pride ourselves on our client success stories and our agile methodologies that adapt to the needs of our community, ensuring every lost item has the best chance of finding its way home.
