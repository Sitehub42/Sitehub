About Lord Montague Pub & Restaurant

Nestled in the bustling area of Montague Gardens, Lord Montague Pub & Restaurant offers a warm and welcoming environment for locals and visitors alike. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable dining experience with a touch of Cape Town's rich culinary history.

Established with a passion for exceptional food and drink, we pride ourselves on our menu featuring locally sourced ingredients and a wide selection of beverages. Our skilled chefs and dedicated staff ensure every visit is memorable, whether you're here for a quick bite or a leisurely meal.

At Lord Montague, we believe in the power of community and hospitality. Our pub and restaurant are more than just a place to eat; they are a gathering space where friendships are forged, and memories are made. We promise to deliver quality and consistency in every dish and drink served.
