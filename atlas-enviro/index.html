About Atlas Enviro

Atlas Enviro is dedicated to the health and preservation of trees within urban and suburban development areas. We serve the Cape Town community by providing detailed assessments and strategies that ensure trees thrive alongside construction projects.

Founded by passionate arborists, our company emerged from a commitment to ecological balance. We work independently from contracting arborists, focusing solely on scientific analysis and conservation methods that benefit both our clients and the environment.

Our promise to our clients is integrity and precision. We provide transparent, data-driven reports and actionable plans that align with statutory compliance, ensuring that your projects progress smoothly without compromising on ecological values.

Our Services

Atlas Enviro offers a comprehensive range of arborist services designed to integrate with and enhance your development projects.

Detailed Tree Surveys

We provide precise tree inventories, including botanical data, structural metrics, and root zone analysis to guide your project’s environmental strategy.

Tree Protection & Monitoring

Our team designs protective measures and monitoring systems to ensure the survival of retained trees during construction activities.

Tree Specification & Replanting

We recommend climate-appropriate species and site-specific plantings to future-proof your landscape with climate-ready trees.

Statutory Compliance

Navigate legal requirements effortlessly with our expertise in the National Forests Act and heritage tree documentation.

Plant Health Care (PHC)

Beyond construction, we manage pest threats and enhance soil health to promote long-term vitality of your trees.
