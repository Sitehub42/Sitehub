About Atlas Enviro

Atlas Enviro specializes in providing independent consulting services on arboriculture. Serving Cape Town and surrounding areas, we are dedicated to preserving the natural beauty of our environment through expert tree management. Our team of specialists ensures that trees thrive in urban settings, balancing the needs of development with ecological preservation.

Founded from a passion for nature and sustainability, Atlas Enviro bridges the gap between construction and environmental conservation. We take pride in our meticulous approach to tree management, offering detailed surveys and protection plans tailored to each project. Our commitment is to the health of trees, providing the technical data and strategies required to manage them safely and effectively.

Our values are rooted in integrity, expertise, and a profound respect for nature. We promise our clients transparent, science-driven solutions that comply with statutory requirements. By choosing Atlas Enviro, you choose a partner dedicated to the ecological future of our urban landscapes.

Our Services

Atlas Enviro offers a comprehensive suite of services to ensure that your development projects harmonize with environmental needs.

Detailed Tree Surveys

We provide precise inventories of trees in development zones, including botanical data, structural metrics, and root analysis for informed decision-making.

Tree Protection & Monitoring

Our team designs and oversees plans for tree survival, offering solutions like protective barriers, root zone protection, and active monitoring.

Tree Specification & Replanting

We recommend climate-appropriate species and site-specific selections to ensure future-proofing and resilience of new or replacement trees.

Statutory Compliance

Our experts navigate legal requirements to keep your projects compliant, avoiding costly delays related to protected species and heritage assessments.

Plant Health Care (PHC)

We implement integrated pest management and soil nutrition strategies to ensure the long-term health and vitality of your trees.

Rapid PSHB Detection

Using a highly sensitive qPCR-based method, we can accurately determine whether a specimen is PSHB
