About Coffee on Orange

Coffee on Orange is a charming cafe nestled in the vibrant streets of Cape Town. We cater to both locals and tourists who are looking for a warm and inviting spot to enjoy high-quality coffee and delicious meals.

Our story began with a passion for creating a cozy and welcoming environment where people can gather and enjoy the simple pleasures of life. Over the years, we've grown into a beloved community hub known for our handcrafted beverages and friendly service.

At Coffee on Orange, we value the trust of our customers and are committed to providing an excellent experience every time you visit. Our promise is to deliver exceptional flavors with every cup of coffee and every meal we serve.
