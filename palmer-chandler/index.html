About Palmer Chandler

At Palmer Chandler, we are passionate about crafting unique and high-quality products that bring serenity and natural beauty into your home. Based in Standford, Western Cape, we specialize in bespoke hand-poured candles and exquisite Bonsai trees.

Our mission is to offer exceptional, organically inspired creations. We pride ourselves on attention to detail, sustainable practices, and an unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. Whether you're looking for a signature scent to elevate your space or a meticulously cultivated Bonsai to add tranquility, we deliver with an agile approach, ensuring every piece meets our exacting standards and brings joy to our customers.

Contact us for wholesale orders to bring Palmer Chandler's distinctive charm to your business.
