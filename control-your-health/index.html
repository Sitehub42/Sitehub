About Control Your Health

Control Your Health is dedicated to providing a comprehensive platform for individuals to securely store and manage their medical history. We cater to those who wish to take charge of their health information and share it effortlessly with healthcare providers.

Founded in Germiston, we have developed an innovative approach that leverages technology to enhance personal healthcare experiences. Our user-friendly platform ensures your medical records are accessible and secure, enabling better healthcare outcomes.

At Control Your Health, we value privacy, security, and innovation. We promise to deliver a service that not only meets your healthcare needs but also empowers you to make informed decisions through seamless access to your medical data.
