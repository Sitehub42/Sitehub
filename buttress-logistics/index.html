About BUTTRESS LOGISTICS

At BUTTRESS LOGISTICS, we are at the forefront of South Africa's logistics revolution, providing cutting-edge solutions to connect manufacturers, warehouses, and consumers. As a leading female-owned logistics company, we leverage technology and strategic precision to deliver unmatched bulk transportation services.

Founded in 2022, BUTTRESS LOGISTICS was established with a vision to empower local industries through innovative and cost-effective logistics solutions. Our journey began with a commitment to integrity, efficiency, and sustainability, continually enhancing our operational capabilities to meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Our core values are centered around delivering reliable services with a customer-focused approach. We are dedicated to fostering a culture of empowerment and innovation, ensuring that every consignment is handled with the utmost care and efficiency.
