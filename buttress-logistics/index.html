About BUTTRESS LOGISTICS

BUTTRESS LOGISTICS is a pioneering force in South Africa's logistics industry, offering cutting-edge transportation solutions. Our core focus is on bulk logistics, seamlessly connecting manufacturers, warehouses, and consumers throughout the region. As a female-owned enterprise, we are proud to lead the charge in transforming how logistics is perceived and executed.

Since our inception in 2022, we have committed to leveraging advanced technology and strategic precision to ensure our clients receive unparalleled service. Our story is one of innovation, as we continuously adapt to the dynamic logistics landscape, ensuring that our fleet operations are at the forefront of efficiency and reliability.

At BUTTRESS LOGISTICS, we operate on principles of Integrity, Efficiency, and Empowerment. Our promise to our customers is a service that embodies Customer Focus, Innovation, and Sustainability. As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to becoming South Africa's leading logistics provider, delivering solutions that empower and uplift local industries.
