About BUTTRESS LOGISTICS

BUTTRESS LOGISTICS is a pioneering force in South Africa's logistics industry, dedicated to providing advanced and efficient bulk transportation solutions. We serve manufacturers, warehouses, and consumers, ensuring seamless connectivity across vital supply chains.

Established in 2022, our female-owned company is committed to revolutionizing logistics through cutting-edge technology and strategic precision. We believe in delivering unmatched value and reliability, empowering local industries with our innovative approach.

Our core values of integrity, efficiency, and empowerment guide every action we take. We promise our customers a focus on innovation and sustainability, ensuring that every logistical challenge is met with the highest standards of service and care.
