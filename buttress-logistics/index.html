About Us

Welcome to Buttress Logistics, South Africa's most advanced female-owned logistics company. We specialize in delivering exceptional bulk transportation solutions that seamlessly connect manufacturers, warehouses, and consumers. Harnessing cutting-edge technology and strategic precision, we ensure that you receive logistics services that are not only efficient but also at the forefront of the industry.

Since our establishment in 2022, our mission has been clear: to provide you with reliable, efficient, and affordable logistics services. We are dedicated to revolutionizing the logistics landscape in South Africa, focusing on bulk transportation solutions that strengthen the nation's economic framework and empower local industries. Our commitment to integrity, efficiency, and empowerment drives us to exceed your expectations every step of the way.

At Buttress Logistics, your needs are our priority. We embrace innovation and sustainability to deliver services that not only meet but surpass industry standards. Join us in fostering a logistics ecosystem that is both innovative and inclusive, where your success is our ultimate goal.
