About Dr Diana Griffiths

Dr Diana Griffiths is dedicated to providing holistic medical care to the residents of Tableview, Cape Town. Our approach focuses on treating the whole person, not just the symptoms. We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming environment where patients can feel comfortable discussing their health concerns.

Our story began with a commitment to bring comprehensive healthcare to our community. With a blend of traditional and alternative medicine, we look beyond conventional treatments to offer our patients a full spectrum of care options. This unique approach allows us to tailor treatments to the individual needs of each patient.

At Dr Diana Griffiths, we value trust, compassion, and integrity. Our goal is to empower patients with knowledge and support, helping them to achieve optimal health and wellness. We promise to listen, understand, and guide you on your journey to better health.
