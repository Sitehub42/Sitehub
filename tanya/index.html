About Us

At Tanya, our mission is to provide exceptional dog walking in Cape Town, ensuring every pet enjoys a stimulating, safe, and joyful experience. We understand that your dog is a cherished family member, and we're committed to their well-being and happiness.

We pride ourselves on our deep expertise in animal behavior and client-centric approach. Utilizing agile methodologies, we adapt our services to meet the unique needs and energy levels of each dog, guaranteeing client success and wagging tails. From energetic pups to senior companions, we tailor our walks to provide the perfect balance of exercise and mental stimulation.
