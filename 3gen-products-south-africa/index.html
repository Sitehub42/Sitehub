About 3Gen Products South Africa

At 3Gen Products South Africa, our mission is to deliver cutting-edge OEM AVN and telematics solutions that seamlessly integrate with our clients' needs, enhancing functionality and user experience. We are committed to innovation, quality, and exceptional customer service, striving to be the trusted partner in automotive technology.

Our expertise lies in tailor-made OEM Audio-Visual Navigation (AVN) systems and accessories, alongside advanced telematics solutions for vehicle tracking, fleet management, and diagnostics. We pride ourselves on agile methodologies, ensuring rapid development and deployment of robust, scalable, and future-proof systems. Our success is directly tied to our clients' success, and we work tirelessly to exceed expectations, providing solutions that drive value and efficiency.

Our Services & Products

At 3Gen Products South Africa, we offer a comprehensive suite of tailor-made OEM AVN and Telematics solutions designed to elevate your driving experience and operational efficiency.

Taylor made OEM AVN and Assesories

Telematics solutions
