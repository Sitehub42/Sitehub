About Cats Grooming

Our mission at Cats Grooming is to provide exceptional, stress-free grooming services for cats, ensuring their health, comfort, and happiness. We believe every cat deserves to look and feel their best.

With years of expertise in feline care, our dedicated groomers specialize in understanding the unique needs and temperaments of cats. We prioritize a calm and gentle approach, using only high-quality, cat-friendly products and techniques. Our commitment to client success is reflected in our personalized services, designed to make each grooming experience as positive and comfortable as possible for your beloved furball. We embrace agile methodologies in our scheduling and service delivery, ensuring flexibility and efficient handling of your cat's grooming requirements.

Our Services

At Cats Grooming, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet your cat's specific grooming needs, from a basic bath to a stylish lion cut. We ensure a safe, clean, and comfortable environment for every feline client.

Full Grooming Package

Includes a thorough bath, gentle blow-dry, brush-out, nail trim, ear cleaning, and de-shedding to leave your cat looking and feeling pristine.

Lion Cut

A stylish and practical option for long-haired cats, featuring a full shave-down to a majestic lion-like mane, complete with a bath.

Nail Trim & Paw Pad Shave

Essential maintenance for healthy paws, preventing discomfort and promoting better hygiene for your cat.

De-shedding Treatment

Specialized treatment to significantly reduce shedding and promote a healthier, shinier coat for your feline friend.

Mat Removal

Gentle and safe removal of mats and tangles, prioritizing your cat's comfort and preventing skin irritation.

Flea & Tick Treatment

Effective and safe solutions to protect your cat from common parasites during their grooming session.
