About The Tiki Tomb

The Tiki Tomb is a vibrant bar and restaurant located in the heart of Cape Town. We pride ourselves on delivering an unforgettable experience through our exceptional service and unique atmosphere. Whether you're local or a visitor, we welcome you with open arms to share in the tiki magic.

Our story began with a desire to create a space where people could relax, enjoy, and indulge in the best cocktails and meals. Our team, led by the amazing Gabbi, is dedicated to providing top-notch service that makes every guest feel special and valued.

At The Tiki Tomb, our values are centered around hospitality, quality, and community. We promise to deliver an experience that not only satisfies your taste buds but also leaves you with fond memories of your time spent with us.
