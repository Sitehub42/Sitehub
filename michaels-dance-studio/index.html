About Us

At Michael's Dance Studio, our mission is simple: 'We will teach you to dance amazingly'. We specialize in West Coast Swing, focusing on personalized instruction that empowers our students. We believe in fostering a supportive and energetic environment, where every dancer can achieve their goals through agile learning methodologies and a commitment to client success.

Our expertise in West Coast Swing dancing ensures that whether you're a complete beginner or looking to refine advanced techniques, you'll receive top-tier guidance. Join us and discover the joy of movement, connection, and amazing dance!

Our Services

Master the art of West Coast Swing with Michael's Dance Studio. Our dedicated classes offer expert guidance, whether you're a beginner or looking to refine your moves. We break down complex steps into easily digestible lessons, ensuring a fun and effective learning experience.

West Coast Swing Dancing

Join our dynamic West Coast Swing classes designed for all levels. Learn fundamental techniques, musicality, and partner connection in a fun and engaging atmosphere.
