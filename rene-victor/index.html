About Rene Victor

Welcome to Rene Victor, where I leverage my 28 years of automotive industry experience to help you find the perfect vehicle. Starting my career as a New Vehicle Sales Executive at Eagle Ford Motors, I have honed my skills to guide you through a seamless car buying journey.

My journey in the automotive world began with a passion for cars and a desire to assist people in making informed decisions. Over the years, I progressed to become a New Ford Car Sales Manager, learning the intricacies of the industry and building strong relationships with clients and dealerships alike.

I am committed to offering exceptional service and trusted guidance, ensuring that every client drives away with confidence and satisfaction. At Rene Victor, your needs and preferences are prioritized to make your vehicle purchase experience enjoyable and stress-free.
