About Step Forward Volunteer Programme Cape Town

Step Forward Volunteer Programme Cape Town is dedicated to consolidating volunteer efforts within the Jewish community in Cape Town. We provide an online platform for new and existing volunteers to sign up and engage with community projects efficiently.

Our initiative was born from a comprehensive review of prior community projects, revealing the need for a centralized volunteer hub. By consulting with 19 organizations and analyzing feedback from 45 participants, we've created a solution that ensures seamless volunteer onboarding and interaction.

We value the safety and development of our volunteers. With rigorous onboarding processes, we ensure that every volunteer is well-prepared and matched with opportunities that align with their skills and interests, fostering a robust volunteer network.

Our Services

We offer a range of services designed to streamline volunteer engagement and enhance community involvement.

Volunteer Onboarding

Our platform provides a self-service onboarding process, ensuring volunteers are well-informed and ready to contribute effectively to community projects.

Volunteer Vetting

We perform thorough vetting processes, including reference checks and interviews, to ensure all volunteers meet community standards and expectations.

Community Network

Access a comprehensive directory of community organizations, complete with detailed mandates and volunteer requirements.

Event Sign-Ups

Organizations can post banner ads and events, allowing volunteers to sign up for opportunities and training directly through our platform.

Administrator Portal

Our global admin portal securely stores volunteer data, facilitating skill matching, succession planning, and strategic volunteer deployment.
