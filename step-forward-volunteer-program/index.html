About Step Forward Volunteer Programme Cape Town

Step Forward Volunteer Programme Cape Town is dedicated to connecting passionate individuals with meaningful volunteer opportunities within the Cape Town community. Our platform simplifies the onboarding process for new volunteers, ensuring a seamless experience from sign-up to engagement.

Our journey began by analyzing the needs of our community through consultations with 19 organizations and a comprehensive survey of 45 individuals. We identified a gap in the volunteer onboarding process and developed a solution that leverages technology to streamline volunteer sign-ups and communications.

We are committed to fostering a safe and inclusive environment for all volunteers. By providing thorough onboarding, training, and professional development, we empower our volunteers to make a positive impact in the community while ensuring their safety and readiness to serve.
