About

At Step Forward Volunteer Programme Cape Town, we are passionately committed to uniting and empowering the Jewish community through a robust volunteer network. Whether you're a new or seasoned volunteer, our intuitive online platform streamlines your journey, enabling you to connect with meaningful community projects effortlessly.

Our program emerged from an in-depth analysis of past initiatives, revealing the need for a centralized volunteer hub. By collaborating with 19 esteemed organizations and gathering insights from 45 dedicated participants, we have crafted a comprehensive solution that ensures smooth and efficient volunteer onboarding and participation.

We place the utmost importance on your safety and growth. Through our rigorous onboarding process, we ensure that you are well-prepared and matched with opportunities that align with your unique skills and interests. Join us in fostering a vibrant and impactful volunteer community, where your contributions truly make a difference.
