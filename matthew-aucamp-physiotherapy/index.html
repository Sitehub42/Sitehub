About Matthew Aucamp Physiotherapy

Matthew Aucamp Physiotherapy is dedicated to providing specialized care in the fields of men's health, musculoskeletal, orthopaedic, and geriatric physiotherapy. Serving the Gqeberha community, we aim to enhance the quality of life for our patients through personalized treatment plans.

Our journey began with a passion for improving patient outcomes through evidence-based practices and a patient-centered approach. We believe in the power of physiotherapy to transform lives, and we work closely with hospitals, old-age homes, and offer flexible home visits and online consultations.

We are committed to delivering compassionate care, focusing on the individual needs of each patient. Our team is dedicated to ongoing professional development to ensure we provide the most effective treatments available.

Our Services

Matthew Aucamp Physiotherapy offers a range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clientele. Each service is designed to address specific health concerns with precision and care.

Men's Health

Specialized physiotherapy focusing on unique male health issues to improve overall well-being and physical functioning.

Musculoskeletal

Comprehensive care for musculoskeletal conditions, promoting recovery through targeted exercises and therapies.

Orthopaedic

Focused treatment plans for orthopaedic injuries and post-surgical rehabilitation aimed at restoring mobility and strength.

Geriatric Care

Physiotherapy services tailored for seniors to maintain independence, manage pain, and enhance quality of life.
