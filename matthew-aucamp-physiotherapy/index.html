About Matthew Aucamp Physiotherapy

Matthew Aucamp Physiotherapy is dedicated to providing comprehensive care focused on men's health, musculoskeletal, orthopaedic, and geriatric needs. Located in Gqeberha, we extend our services to hospitals, old-age homes, and offer home visits and online consultations to ensure flexibility and convenience for our valued clients.

Founded by Matthew Aucamp, our practice has developed a reputation for patient-centered care that is both compassionate and effective. Our approach combines the latest physiotherapeutic techniques with a personalized touch, ensuring each treatment plan is tailored to meet the unique needs of each patient.

Our mission is to empower individuals through improved mobility and pain management, promoting overall well-being and enhancing quality of life. We believe in treating every patient with dignity, respect, and the utmost professionalism.

Our Services

At Matthew Aucamp Physiotherapy, we offer a range of specialized services to cater to diverse physiotherapy needs, ensuring effective treatment and recovery.

Men's Health

Our men's health physiotherapy services address specific issues like pelvic pain, incontinence, and post-prostate surgery recovery, tailored to enhance quality of life.

Musculoskeletal Care

We provide comprehensive treatment for musculoskeletal conditions, focusing on pain relief, injury recovery, and improving physical function.

Orthopaedic Rehabilitation

Our orthopaedic rehabilitation services help patients recover from surgeries, fractures, and other injuries, restoring strength and mobility.

Geriatric Care

We offer specialized geriatric physiotherapy to address age-related conditions, focusing on improving balance, coordination, and overall mobility.
