About Biltong Bar

Biltong Bar is dedicated to bringing the authentic taste of premium homemade biltong to the heart of Cape Town. We cater to food enthusiasts who appreciate the nuanced flavors of expertly cured meats.

Our journey began with a simple love for traditional South African cuisine. Over the years, we've honed our craft, using only the finest ingredients and time-honored techniques to produce biltong that is both delicious and memorable.

At Biltong Bar, we value quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction above all else. We promise to deliver a product that not only meets but exceeds your expectations, every time.
