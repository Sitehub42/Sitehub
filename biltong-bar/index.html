About Biltong bar

Biltong bar is a Cape Town-based company dedicated to bringing the rich and savory flavors of traditional South African biltong to our customers. Whether you're a local or a visitor, our biltong is crafted to offer you a taste of home or a new culinary adventure.

Our journey began with a passion for authentic biltong, inspired by time-honored recipes and methods. We carefully source the highest quality meats, ensuring that each bite is packed with flavor and nutrition. Our team is committed to preserving the cultural heritage of biltong while embracing modern techniques to enhance its taste.

At Biltong bar, our values are rooted in quality, authenticity, and customer satisfaction. We promise to deliver biltong that not only delights your taste buds but also supports sustainable practices and the local community. Join us in celebrating the unique flavors of South Africa.
