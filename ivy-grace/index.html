About Ivy Grace

At Ivy Grace, we are passionate about crafting unique handmade jewellery and handbags, meticulously designed from recycled materials. Our creations are perfect for those who cherish both artistry and sustainability, offering you a blend of elegance and eco-consciousness.

Founded in the vibrant heart of Cape Town, Ivy Grace emerged from a vision to transform discarded items into stunning, wearable art. Each piece we create is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality and creativity, ensuring that you receive products that are as exceptional as they are sustainable.

We invite you to experience the power of transformation and the allure of the unexpected. Our mission is to provide you with products that not only champion eco-friendly principles but also reflect your individual style and sophistication. Discover elegance reimagined with Ivy Grace.
