About License to Grill Restaurant Durbanville

At License to Grill Restaurant Durbanville, we offer a unique dining experience that caters to both adults and children. Our restaurant is divided into two sections: an upstairs section for our 18+ patrons to enjoy drinks, and a downstairs section dedicated to dining. Our outside play area ensures the little ones are entertained while you relax and enjoy your meal.

Our story began with a simple mission: to provide a cozy and welcoming environment where families and friends can gather for great food and good times. We pride ourselves on our friendly and attentive staff, who are always ready to make your visit memorable. Our menu is carefully crafted to offer something for everyone, from our famous Bond Burger to a range of delicious dishes that will tantalize your taste buds.

We value quality, comfort, and community. At License to Grill, we promise to deliver impeccable service and a warm atmosphere every time you visit. Whether you're a regular or a first-time guest, we aim to make your experience with us a delightful one. Come and see why we're a favorite spot in Durbanville.
