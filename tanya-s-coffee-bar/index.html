About Tanya's Coffee Bar

Tanya's Coffee Bar, nestled in the heart of Cape Town, is your destination for the finest coffee, teas, and decadent cakes. We serve coffee aficionados and casual sippers alike, ensuring every visit is a delightful experience.

Founded by Tanya, a passionate coffee enthusiast, our café offers a unique blend of tradition and innovation. From handpicked coffee beans to our artisanal brewing methods, we take pride in every cup we serve.

At Tanya's Coffee Bar, customer satisfaction is our top priority. We promise a warm atmosphere, friendly service, and the best brews that Cape Town has to offer.
