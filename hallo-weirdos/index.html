About Us

At Hallo-Weirdos, we believe every dog deserves to shine (or spook!) on Halloween. We craft unique, high-quality, made-to-order costumes that capture the spirit of the season.

Our mission is simple: to provide unparalleled Halloween outfits for dogs that are as unique as your beloved pet. All products are made to order with a 4-week lead time, ensuring meticulous attention to detail and a perfect fit. Please note, delivery price is not included in the costume cost.

Specializing in creative and fun Halloween designs, we bring your spooky visions to life. Our agile approach to design and production means we can adapt to unique requests, ensuring each costume is crafted with precision and care, always aiming for paw-fect client satisfaction. We're dedicated to helping you create unforgettable Halloween memories with your four-legged family members.
