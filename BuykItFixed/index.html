About Us

At Buyk It Fixed, our mission is simple: Bicycle repair. We are dedicated to providing top-notch repair and maintenance services, ensuring your ride is always smooth, safe, and enjoyable. Specializing in all aspects of bicycle repair within the Services industry, our expertise covers everything from minor adjustments to major overhauls.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through transparent communication, reliable service, and a passion for cycling. We emphasize client success by meticulously diagnosing issues and employing agile methodologies to deliver efficient and effective solutions, getting you back on the road in no time.

Our Services

From routine check-ups to complex repairs, Buyk It Fixed offers a comprehensive range of services to keep your bicycle in peak condition. We handle all types of bikes and issues with precision and care.

Tire Repair & Puncture Fixes

Quick and reliable tire repairs, tube replacements, and puncture fixes to get you rolling again.

Brake Adjustments & Replacements

Ensure your safety with perfectly tuned brakes, including pad replacements and cable adjustments.

Gear Tuning & Derailleur Service

Smooth shifting is essential. We fine-tune your gears and service derailleurs for optimal performance.

Full Bike Servicing & Tune-ups

Comprehensive inspection, cleaning, lubrication, and adjustment of all major components.

Accessory Installation

Professional installation of new lights, racks, fenders, computers, and other bicycle accessories.

Custom Builds & Upgrades

Planning a custom build or looking to upgrade components? We offer expert advice and installation.
