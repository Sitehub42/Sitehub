About Walk This Way

Our mission: To provide reliable, loving, and professional pet care services tailored to the unique needs of every animal.

At Walk This Way, we are dedicated to providing exceptional pet care services in the Pet Care Services industry. We pride ourselves on creating lasting relationships built on trust and a genuine love for animals. We emphasize client success by ensuring every pet receives personalized attention and care.

All products are made to order with a 4 week lead time, delivery price not included.

We embrace agile methodologies in our service delivery, ensuring flexibility and responsiveness to your pet's evolving needs, always striving for the highest standards of care and client satisfaction.

Our Services

Walk This Way offers a range of professional pet care services designed to keep your furry friends happy and healthy. From daily walks to extended stays, we've got your pet's needs covered with love and expertise.

Dog Walking

Enjoy peace of mind knowing your dog is getting the exercise and attention they need with our tailored dog walking services.

Pet Sitting (Hourly)

Perfect for short periods away. We provide dedicated care, feeding, and playtime for your pets in the comfort of their own home.

Pet Sitting (Stay Over)

For longer trips, our overnight pet sitting ensures your pets maintain their routine in a familiar environment, receiving constant companionship.
