Our Core Business

At Keyboard Repairs, our core business revolves around providing unparalleled solutions for all your keyboard needs. While specializing in expert repair services, we also offer a curated selection of quality hardware sales, ensuring you have access to the best components and accessories. We're committed to restoring your typing experience with precision and care.

Our Services & Products

Hardware Sales

Explore our wide selection of high-quality keyboard components, replacement parts, and accessories. From keycaps and switches to custom cables and ergonomic add-ons, we provide everything you need to upgrade or maintain your perfect typing setup.

Expert Repairs

Don't let a broken keyboard slow you down. Our skilled technicians specialize in diagnosing and fixing a wide range of keyboard issues, including sticky keys, non-responsive switches, liquid damage, and more. We bring your beloved keyboard back to life.

Custom Builds

Dreaming of a unique keyboard tailored to your exact specifications? We offer custom keyboard building services, helping you select the perfect case, PCB, switches, and keycaps to create your ultimate personalized typing machine.
