About Us

Our Mission: Makes great hammocks for the family.

Bananas In Pajamas is a leader in the manufacturing of high-quality consumer goods, specifically specializing in comfortable and durable hammocks designed for family relaxation. With a focus on innovation and comfort, we ensure every hammock provides unparalleled leisure for you and your loved ones.

We combine robust manufacturing processes with a deep understanding of customer needs to deliver products that are not only aesthetically pleasing but also built to last.
