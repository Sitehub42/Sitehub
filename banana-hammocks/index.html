About Us

At Bananas In Pajamas, our mission is to make great hammocks for the family, ensuring comfort, durability, and style for every home.

We are a dedicated manufacturing company specializing in high-quality consumer goods, with a particular focus on innovative and comfortable hammocks. Our commitment to craftsmanship and customer satisfaction drives everything we do, from material selection to the final stitch.
