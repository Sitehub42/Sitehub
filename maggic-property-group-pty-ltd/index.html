About Maggic Property Group (PTY) Ltd

Maggic Property Group (PTY) Ltd is dedicated to providing personalized property management services to the residents of Cape Town. With a keen understanding of the local market, we strive to offer solutions that cater to the unique needs of each client, ensuring their properties are well-maintained and managed.

Our journey began with a mission to bring a personal touch to property management. We believe in building relationships with our clients, offering transparency, and always prioritizing their needs. This commitment has allowed us to grow and become a trusted name in the industry.

At Maggic Property Group, our core values are integrity, communication, and excellence. We promise to treat each property as if it were our own, ensuring that every detail is handled with care and precision. Our team is always available to answer questions and provide support, making your property management experience as seamless as possible.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive property management services designed to give you peace of mind and ensure the smooth operation of your property.

Property Management

Our property management service handles everything from tenant screening to maintenance, ensuring your investment is protected and profitable. We provide detailed financial reporting and regular property inspections to keep you informed and your property in top condition.
