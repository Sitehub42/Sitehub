About Select Beauty Studio

Select Beauty Studio is your local destination for premium hair and beauty services in Milnerton, Cape Town. We specialize in a wide range of hair treatments, from classic cuts to modern balayage, and use only the finest products to ensure the best results for our clients.

Founded by passionate stylists, our studio has become a trusted name in the community for its commitment to quality and personalized care. We believe that every client deserves a unique experience that caters to their individual style and preferences.

At Select Beauty Studio, we pride ourselves on creating a warm and welcoming environment where clients can relax and enjoy a moment of pampering. Our team is dedicated to helping you look and feel your best, and we promise to deliver exceptional service with every visit.
