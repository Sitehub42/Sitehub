Our Services

Project 360 offers a tiered approach to project and time management, designed to scale with your business needs. Choose the plan that best fits your team and operations.

Starter Pack

Perfect for individuals or small teams focused solely on tracking work hours. Our Starter pack provides essential timekeeping features to accurately log and manage time spent on tasks.

Standard Plan

For growing teams needing comprehensive tools for both timekeeping and project management. The Standard plan combines robust time tracking with features for task assignment, progress monitoring, and project collaboration.

Enterprise Solution

Tailored for large businesses and corporations requiring an extensive, custom enterprise setup. This solution offers advanced features, dedicated support, and scalable infrastructure to meet complex organizational demands.
