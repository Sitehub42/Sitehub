About Patricia Blake

At Patricia Blake, our mission is to provide compassionate, evidence-based clinical psychology services tailored to the unique needs of older adolescents and adults. We are dedicated to fostering mental well-being, resilience, and personal growth for all our clients.

With extensive expertise in clinical psychology, Patricia Blake specializes in addressing a wide range of mental health concerns, including anxiety, depression, stress management, and relationship issues. We employ evidence-based therapeutic approaches, ensuring the most effective and personalized care for adolescents and adults navigating life's challenges.

Client success is at the heart of our practice. We believe in empowering individuals to develop healthy coping mechanisms, strengthen relationships, and achieve lasting positive change.

Our Services

Patricia Blake offers a comprehensive range of clinical psychology services designed to support older adolescents, individuals, and couples in their journey towards mental wellness.

Individual Therapy

Personalized one-on-one sessions for older adolescents and adults to explore concerns, develop coping strategies, and foster personal growth.

Couples Counseling

Support for couples to improve communication, resolve conflicts, and strengthen their relationship bonds.

Adolescent Psychology

Specialized care for older adolescents facing developmental, emotional, or behavioral challenges as they transition to adulthood.

Anxiety & Depression Support

Compassionate and effective therapeutic approaches to help individuals manage anxiety and depression.

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Evidence-based techniques to identify and modify unhelpful thinking patterns and behaviors.

Relationship Therapy

Guidance for individuals and couples to navigate relationship challenges and build healthier connections.
