About Michelle Glover Physiotherapist

I am Michelle Glover, a dedicated physiotherapist in Gqeberha, passionate about helping you regain control over your body. Specializing in pain management and functional rehabilitation, my mission is to empower you to live a life free from pain.

With a unique approach that combines dry needling, kinesiology, and sports injury management, I tailor each session to fit your individual needs. My journey into physiotherapy began with a desire to understand the science of movement and its impact on overall health.

My commitment to your well-being is reflected in every treatment plan I create. By focusing on holistic and personalized care, I promise to guide you towards a healthier, more active lifestyle.

Our Services

Explore the comprehensive services designed to cater to your specific physiotherapy needs.

General Physiotherapy

Providing personalized treatment plans to enhance mobility and relieve pain, ensuring a swift recovery and better quality of life.

Dry Needling

Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehabilitation programs tailored to help you recover from injuries and surgeries, restoring strength and function.

Kinesio Taping

Utilizing kinesio taping to support joints and muscles, reduce pain, and enhance performance during physical activities.

Sports Injury

Pain Management

Integrated pain management strategies aimed at reducing chronic pain and improving your overall functionality and quality of life.
