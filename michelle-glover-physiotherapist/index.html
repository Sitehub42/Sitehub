About Michelle Glover Physiotherapist

I am Michelle Glover, a dedicated physiotherapist committed to helping you regain your strength, mobility, and overall well-being. My practice is based in Gqeberha, where I offer a range of specialized services tailored to meet your specific needs.

My journey into physiotherapy began with a deep passion for helping others. After completing my studies in physiotherapy, I gained extensive experience in various healthcare settings, which equipped me with the skills to provide comprehensive care. I believe in a holistic approach to rehabilitation, ensuring that all aspects of my clients' health are considered.

At the core of my practice is a commitment to providing personalized care. I strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where you feel supported and understood. Together, we will work towards achieving your health goals, whether it's recovering from an injury, managing pain, or improving your athletic performance.

Our Services

Explore a variety of physiotherapy services designed to enhance your health and well-being.

General Physiotherapy

Receive comprehensive assessments and treatments tailored to improve your overall physical function and alleviate pain.

Dry Needling

Experience targeted muscle release through dry needling, promoting healing and reducing muscle tension.

Rehabilitation

Engage in personalized rehabilitation programs to aid recovery and restore optimal movement post-injury.

Kinesio Taping

Enhance your movement and support your body with kinesio taping techniques that facilitate recovery.

Sports Injury

Benefit from specialized sports therapy to address and prevent injuries, ensuring you stay active and healthy.

Pain Management

Work with me to manage chronic pain effectively, enhancing your quality of life through tailored strategies.
