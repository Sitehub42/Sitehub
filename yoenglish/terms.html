Terms and Conditions

Bookings

  • To book lessons, please contact us on WeChat.
  • Students may schedule fixed classes at the same time every week.

Cancellations

  • If class is cancelled 24 hours in advance, there will be no penalty. You will not lose the class if it is part of a package. However, if you know that you need to cancel a class, we would be grateful if you could cancel as far ahead as possible.
  • If a class is cancelled less than 24 hours in advance, the class will be forfeited.
  • To allow for illness or unexpected emergencies, a class may be cancelled in less than 24 hours once per calendar month without penalty.

Lateness

  • The teacher will remain in the classroom for the full 25 minutes of class time.
  • Should the student arrive late, the remaining time will be used to teach.

Absence

If the student misses a class, it will still be deducted from your package of lessons.
