About YoEnglish

At YoEnglish, our mission is to provide an immersive and joyful online English learning experience for children aged 4 to 16. We are dedicated to fostering strong foundational language skills, perfect pronunciation, and a lifelong love for English through personalized 1:1 tutoring from expert UK and USA teachers, guided by the renowned Oxford University Press Starlight curriculum.

Our live English lessons are designed for maximum engagement and effectiveness:

  • Specifically for children aged 4 to 16.
  • Online, creating an exciting, interactive experience with songs, stories, and videos.
  • 1:1 tutoring for high-quality, personalized learning by expert teachers from the UK and USA.
  • Structured on the Starlight curriculum from Oxford University Press.
  • Reinforced with homework, together with audio tracks to ensure perfect pronunciation.
  • Each lesson is 25 minutes long, perfectly suited for young attention spans.

We pride ourselves on an agile teaching methodology, adapting to each child's unique pace and learning style, ensuring client success and confidence in their English journey.
