About Mario's Italian Restaurant

Located in the heart of Green Point, Cape Town, Mario's Italian Restaurant is a cozy haven for lovers of authentic Italian cuisine. Our menu is a celebration of the finest ingredients and traditional recipes, offering a taste of Italy right here in the local community.

Our journey began with a passion for delivering not just exceptional food, but memorable dining experiences. From our signature salmon pasta to the thin prosciutto pizza with perfectly burnt edges, every dish is crafted with love and served with a smile.

At Mario's, we believe in the power of good food to bring people together. Our walls are adorned with heartfelt feedback from diners, a testament to the warmth and quality that we strive to deliver every day. Join us for a meal and become part of our family.
