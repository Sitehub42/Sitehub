About Quarter Cuppa

Quarter Cuppa is a beloved cake shop nestled in the heart of Cape Town, serving delicious cakes to the local community. With a passion for creating the perfect cake for every occasion, we pride ourselves on using secret family recipes that have been passed down through generations, ensuring every bite is a blissful experience.

Our story began with a simple love for baking and a dream to share our passion with others. Over the years, we have perfected our craft, dedicating ourselves to producing the finest cakes that bring joy and smiles to our customers. Our shop is a place of warmth and happiness where each cake is made with love and attention to detail.

At Quarter Cuppa, we believe in the power of a good cake to make any moment special. Our promise is to provide our customers with cakes that are not only visually stunning but also delightfully flavorful, made with the freshest ingredients. We are committed to making your celebrations memorable with our scrumptious creations.
