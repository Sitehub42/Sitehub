About Ashleigh White Physiotherapy

As a dedicated physiotherapist, I am passionate about helping individuals regain their strength and mobility. My practice is located in the heart of Durban North, where I provide tailored treatments to ensure that each client's unique needs are met. With a focus on holistic healing, I aim to guide my clients through their recovery journey with empathy and expertise.

My journey into physiotherapy began with a fascination for the human body's ability to heal and adapt. After years of study and practice, I established my own clinic to create a space where patients feel supported and empowered. My approach is rooted in evidence-based techniques and personalized care plans, ensuring the best possible outcomes.

At Ashleigh White Physiotherapy, my core values are integrity, compassion, and excellence in care. I am committed to providing a nurturing environment where clients feel listened to and respected. Your well-being is my top priority, and I strive to help you achieve your health goals with confidence and support.
