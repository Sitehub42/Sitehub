About Tanya's Art

Tanya's Art is dedicated to creating captivating oil paintings and pencil drawings that transform any space into a gallery of emotions and stories. Based in South Africa, we cater to art enthusiasts and collectors who appreciate the finer things in life.

Our journey began with a passion for capturing the essence of beauty through art. Tanya, our founder, has been painting for over a decade, exploring various themes and styles to create pieces that resonate with the soul. Each artwork is a testament to her commitment to excellence and creativity.

At Tanya's Art, we believe that art should evoke emotion and inspire. We are committed to delivering high-quality, original artworks that bring joy and inspiration to our clients. Our promise is to provide art that not only decorates but also enriches your life.
