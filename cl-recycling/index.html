About CL Recycling

CL Recycling is dedicated to providing efficient and responsible scrap and E-Waste collection services. We specialize in convenient onsite weighing and immediate payment, ensuring a seamless experience for our clients. Our expertise in the recycling industry, combined with an agile approach, focuses on maximizing client success and contributing to a sustainable future. We are committed to fostering environmental sustainability through accessible and reliable recycling solutions.

Our Services

At CL Recycling, we offer comprehensive recycling solutions designed to make responsible disposal easy and rewarding. Our key services include:

Scrap Metal Collections

We efficiently collect various types of scrap metal from your location. Our team ensures proper sorting and handling, providing competitive rates and instant payment on site.

E-Waste Collections

Dispose of your electronic waste responsibly with CL Recycling. We handle computers, monitors, batteries, and other e-waste, preventing harmful materials from entering landfills. Onsite weighing and payment for qualifying items.

Onsite Weighing & Payment

Experience convenience with our onsite weighing and payment service. We provide transparent and fair evaluations for your scrap and e-waste, with immediate payment, making the process effortless for you.

Scheduled Collections

We offer flexible scheduling options for regular scrap and e-waste collections. Whether you need weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly pickups, we tailor our services to fit your needs.

Ferrous & Non‑Ferrous Metal Testing

On-site testing and verification of ferrous and non‑ferrous metals for accurate grading and pricing.
