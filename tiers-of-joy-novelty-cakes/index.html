About Tiers of Joy

At Tiers of Joy Novelty Cakes, I believe every celebration deserves a unique and delicious centerpiece. I am a home-based baker dedicated to crafting custom novelty cakes and cupcakes, meticulously designed to bring your vision to life.

My mission is simple: to create edible works of art that not only look incredible but taste divine. From whimsical birthday creations to elegant wedding tiers, each cake is a bespoke masterpiece, reflecting your personal style and the joy of your occasion. I pride myself on attention to detail, high-quality ingredients, and a passion for turning your sweet dreams into a reality.

With Tiers of Joy, you're not just getting a cake; you're getting a personalized experience from concept to creation, ensuring your celebration is truly unforgettable.
