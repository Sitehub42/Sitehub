About Shells for Africa

Shells for Africa is dedicated to providing a diverse range of quality shells for collectors and enthusiasts throughout Johannesburg. Our carefully curated collections are perfect for both personal and commercial use, ensuring that every shell meets our high standards of quality and uniqueness.

Founded in 2010, Shells for Africa started as a small family business. Over the years, we have expanded our reach and cultivated relationships with trusted suppliers worldwide. Our journey has been driven by a passion for the natural beauty of shells and a dedication to sharing that beauty with our customers.

We believe in sustainable and ethical sourcing. Our promise is to deliver exceptional products and a personalized service to all our customers. We are committed to ensuring that every interaction with Shells for Africa is a positive and fulfilling experience.

Our Services

At Shells for Africa, we offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of our diverse clientele. Whether you're looking for unique shell pieces or bulk orders, we have the expertise and resources to fulfill your requirements.

Shell Sales

Our extensive collection includes rare and beautiful shells ideal for collectors, decorators, and hobbyists. Each shell is handpicked to ensure quality and authenticity.

Wholesale Distribution

We offer bulk purchasing options for businesses and individuals. Our streamlined distribution network ensures timely delivery of large orders across Johannesburg and beyond.

Eco-Friendly Sourcing

Committed to sustainability, we ensure all our shells are sourced responsibly, with minimal environmental impact, supporting conservation efforts worldwide.
