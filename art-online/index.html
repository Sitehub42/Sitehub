About Art online

Hello, I'm Tanya Kannemeyer, a passionate art instructor dedicated to bringing the joy of art into your home. At Art online, I offer a range of lessons for both kids and adults, focusing on various mediums to inspire creativity and self-expression. Whether you're just starting or looking to refine your skills, I provide personalized guidance tailored to your needs.

My journey with art began at a young age, and over the years, I have honed my skills across different artistic disciplines. After seeing the transformative power of art, I decided to share my knowledge with others through online teaching. This allows me to reach students globally, making art accessible to everyone.

At Art online, I believe in fostering a nurturing and encouraging environment. My goal is to help you explore your artistic potential while enjoying the process of creating. I promise to deliver engaging lessons that not only teach you technique but also ignite your passion for art.
