About Me

Natalie's House Sitting offers dedicated house and pet sitting services to homeowners in the vibrant city of Cape Town. Whether you're traveling for a month or longer, you can trust us to look after your home and furry friends with the utmost care.

Founded on a love for animals and a commitment to excellent service, Natalie's House Sitting emerged from the need for reliable home care services in the area. We understand the importance of having peace of mind while you're away, and we strive to provide just that.

Our core values include trust, reliability, and compassion. We promise to treat your home and pets as if they were our own, ensuring they receive the care and attention they deserve. Your satisfaction and peace of mind are our top priorities.
