About Natalie's House Sitting

Natalie's House Sitting provides a unique service for those needing long-term care for their homes and pets in the Cape Town area. Whether you're traveling for work or pleasure, we ensure your home remains in the safe hands of a dedicated professional.

Founded out of a passion for animal care and home management, Natalie has combined her love for animals and her experience in home maintenance to create a service that offers peace of mind for her clients. Her approach is personal and attentive, ensuring each house and pet receives tailored care.

Our core values of reliability, trust, and empathy drive everything we do. Natalie is committed to providing a service that not only meets but exceeds the expectations of her clients, ensuring they return to a well-maintained home and happy pets.
