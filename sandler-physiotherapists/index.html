About Sandler Physiotherapists

I am a dedicated physiotherapist based in Durban North, South Africa, committed to helping you achieve your health and wellness goals. With years of experience and a passion for healing, I provide personalized care tailored to each individual's needs.

My journey began with a strong desire to make a difference in people's lives through physiotherapy. Over the years, I have honed my skills and expanded my knowledge to offer a comprehensive range of services that address various physical health concerns.

At Sandler Physiotherapists, I value empathy, integrity, and a client-centered approach. I promise to work closely with you to develop effective treatment plans that promote recovery and enhance your quality of life.
