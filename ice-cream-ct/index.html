About Ice cream CT

Ice cream CT is a local favorite in Cape Town, known for its artisanal ice cream made with the freshest ingredients. We serve a diverse clientele ranging from families to foodies, all of whom appreciate the quality and creativity of our flavors.

Founded by a passionate ice cream enthusiast, our journey began with a simple mission: to bring joy and sweetness to every scoop. Over the years, we have perfected our recipes, ensuring each flavor is an explosion of taste and texture.

Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We promise our customers not just ice cream, but an experience that delights the senses. From classic vanilla to exotic mango, every flavor is crafted to perfection.
