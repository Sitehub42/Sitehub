About Ice cream CT

Ice cream CT is your go-to destination for a delightful assortment of ice cream flavors, crafted with love in the heart of Cape Town. We cater to ice cream enthusiasts of all ages, ensuring each scoop is a moment of joy.

Founded by a passionate team of ice cream aficionados, Ice cream CT was born from the desire to bring gourmet ice cream experiences to the local community. Our approach is simple: use the finest ingredients and traditional methods to create flavors that surprise and delight.

At Ice cream CT, we value quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction above all else. Our promise is to deliver an unforgettable ice cream experience, with every visit leaving you craving more.
