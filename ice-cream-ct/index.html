About Ice cream CT

Ice cream CT is your local haven for indulgent, handcrafted ice creams. Located in the heart of Cape Town, we serve a diverse community of ice cream enthusiasts looking for unique and delicious flavors. Our mission is to bring joy and sweetness to every customer through our artisanal creations.

Our journey began with a passion for quality ingredients and a love for the art of ice cream making. Over the years, we have perfected our recipes to ensure that every scoop is a delightful experience. We pride ourselves on using locally sourced ingredients to create flavors that are both innovative and nostalgic.

At Ice cream CT, we believe in putting our customers first. We are committed to providing exceptional service and a welcoming atmosphere where everyone can enjoy our treats. Our promise is to continue bringing you the finest ice cream with a smile.
