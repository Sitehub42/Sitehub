About Ice cream CT

Ice cream CT is dedicated to delighting the taste buds of Cape Town residents with our wide array of ice cream flavors. Our mission is to bring joy and sweetness to every occasion, serving everyone from families to corporate events.

Founded in the heart of Cape Town, our story began with a passion for creating the finest ice cream using only the freshest ingredients. Our approach is simple: quality over quantity, ensuring each scoop is a masterpiece.

We value customer satisfaction above all else, promising a delightful experience with every visit. Our commitment to quality and flavor innovation sets us apart in the ice cream industry.

Our Services

At Ice cream CT, we offer an extensive range of unique and classic ice cream flavors, catering to every taste and preference. Whether you're in the mood for something traditional or adventurous, we have the perfect scoop for you.

All Flavours

Our ice cream selection includes a variety of flavors, from classic vanilla and chocolate to exotic mango and matcha. Each flavor is crafted with care to ensure a rich and creamy experience.
