About Ice cream CT

Ice cream CT is dedicated to crafting the finest ice cream using locally sourced ingredients. We serve the vibrant community of Cape Town, offering a wide array of flavors that cater to every palate. Our mission is to bring joy and sweetness to every occasion with our delicious ice cream.

Founded in the heart of Cape Town, Ice cream CT began as a small family-run business. Our passion for quality and creativity has driven us to expand our offerings and reach more ice cream enthusiasts. We believe in the power of a good scoop to brighten your day.

At Ice cream CT, we value quality, community, and innovation. Our promise is to deliver exceptional ice cream that not only tastes great but also supports local producers. We are committed to sustainability and strive to make a positive impact in our community.
