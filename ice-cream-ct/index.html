About Ice cream CT

Ice cream CT is your local haven for delightful ice cream flavors, serving the vibrant community of Cape Town. Our mission is to bring joy and sweetness to your day with our handcrafted ice creams, made fresh daily using the finest ingredients.

Our story began with a passion for creating unique and flavorful ice creams that cater to every palate. From classic vanilla to exotic tropical blends, we pride ourselves on offering a diverse range of flavors that celebrate the rich culinary heritage of our region.

At Ice cream CT, we value quality, creativity, and customer satisfaction. We are committed to providing an exceptional ice cream experience that not only tantalizes your taste buds but also supports local farmers and producers. Join us in our journey to spread happiness, one scoop at a time.
