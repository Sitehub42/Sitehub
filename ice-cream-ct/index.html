About Ice cream CT

Ice cream CT is dedicated to serving the finest quality ice cream in Cape Town. Our customers range from local residents to tourists looking for a sweet treat to enjoy the vibrant city. We offer a variety of flavors to cater to every taste, ensuring that every scoop is a delightful experience.

Founded with the passion for creating exceptional ice cream, Ice cream CT has quickly become a local favorite. Our approach is simple: use the freshest ingredients and traditional methods to create a product that is both authentic and delicious. We are proud to contribute to the local economy by sourcing ingredients from nearby farms.

Our promise to our customers is unwavering quality and service. We believe in creating memorable moments with every scoop, whether it's a family outing or a solo indulgence. At Ice cream CT, we are committed to bringing joy to our community one cone at a time.
