About Ice cream CT

Ice cream CT is dedicated to serving the finest ice cream in Cape Town. Our selection includes a wide variety of flavors, each crafted with the freshest ingredients to ensure a delightful experience with every scoop.

Founded in the heart of Cape Town, Ice cream CT began as a passion project to bring joy to our community through delicious treats. Our team is committed to quality and innovation, constantly exploring new flavors and techniques.

At Ice cream CT, our promise is to deliver exceptional service and unforgettable flavors. We believe in creating a welcoming environment for all ice cream lovers, where everyone can find their favorite flavor or discover a new one.
