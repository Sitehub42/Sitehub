About Peachy Tea

Our mission at Peachy Tea is to craft and deliver the most delightful and refreshing homemade ice teas, using only the finest ingredients to bring a taste of peachy perfection to every sip. We are passionate about creating unique and refreshing experiences for our clients, focusing on quality, creativity, and a touch of peachy joy. Our agile approach ensures that we can quickly adapt to client preferences and market trends, consistently delivering excellence.

With Peachy Tea, you're not just getting a drink; you're getting a handcrafted beverage made with love. We specialize in flavoured homemade ice teas that are perfect for any occasion, from a quiet afternoon to a lively gathering. Our commitment to client success means we listen to your needs, whether it's for a custom blend or a bulk order, ensuring every sip brings satisfaction.
