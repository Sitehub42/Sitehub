About Wellness Steam & Sauna (PTY) Ltd trading as Steamwellness

Wellness Steam & Sauna specializes in manufacturing robust Steamflo R steam generators, serving diverse needs from home conversions to luxury spa installations. Our custom-built generators are ideal for various applications, including Hammams and Rasuls.

With over four decades of research and development, we pride ourselves on the quality and reliability of our products. As local manufacturers, we ensure our components are sourced locally, making parts readily available and supporting after-sales service and upgrades.

Our commitment is to provide certified, high-quality steam generators with a focus on customer satisfaction. We have established distributors nationwide and export to Europe and Africa, ensuring our reach extends globally.
